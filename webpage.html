The Porsche 911

About

Porsche 911s—just when you think you’ve figured them out, another model comes along with a new set of letters and numbers. Carrera, Turbo, GT3, Targa... wait, what’s the difference again? The lineup has more variations than there are shades of gray on the paint palette. But don’t worry! We’re here to help untangle the mystery. Whether you’re a seasoned Porsche enthusiast or just starting your journey into the world of 911s, we’ve got you covered with a simple guide to the different design variations. Let’s break down the Porsche 911 lineup and make sense of all those letters and numbers together!
