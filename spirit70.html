Highlights

Quotes of an era

Bringing the 70s back with the Olive Neo as the body color and the decorative stripes on the hood. The windshield frame in Satin Black and Bronzite on the wheels, front end and the rear fascia add more accentuating details

Pasha

The pulsating, futuristic pattern adorns features such as the seat centers. the door center panels, the glove box and the reversible luggage compartment mat.

Sport Classic wheels

Based on the Fuchsfelge (fix wheel), the 20-/21-inch Sport Classic wheels, painted in Black (satin gloss) and Brontize, with central locking, combine high performance and classic aesthetics

The crest from 1963

A unifying element. The historic crest from 1963 is embossed on the hood, wheels, key and steering wheel, as well as the headrests and keycase.

Only 1500 units

The gold-colored limited edition badge with '911' lettering, model designation and personal limited edition number gracing the dashboard decorative trim of every 911 Spirit 70 identifies it as a collector's item.
